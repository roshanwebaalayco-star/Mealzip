Sarvam for Voice — KEEP IT
Do not drop Sarvam. Here's why it's viable and important:

YOLOv11 Custom Model — DROP IT (for now)
Be honest with yourselves: training a custom object detection model requires weeks of data collection, labeling, and GPU training time. You don't have that before April 18.

The good news: You don't need it. Gemini Vision (the same API you're already using) can identify ingredients from a photo extremely well — paneer, dal, atta, vegetables, everything. For the demo, Gemini Vision doing pantry scanning is equally impressive and actually works reliably.

Plan: use Gemini Vision now, plan the custom YOLOv11 model as a future milestone after the hackathon when you have time to collect real kitchen photos from Indian homes.

Your Competitive Advantage Over Gemini and ChatGPT
This is important to understand clearly, because judges will ask this.

Gemini and ChatGPT are general-purpose chat tools. You are a specialized system. The difference:

1. Family unit vs. individual
You cannot easily ask ChatGPT: "Plan one meal for a diabetic dad, a weight-loss mom, and a 8-year-old child who all cook in one kitchen on a ₹5,000 budget in Bokaro in March." And even if it answers once, it doesn't remember next week. ParivarSehat does this automatically, every time, and gets better with feedback.

2. India-specific knowledge is built in, not prompted
ChatGPT doesn't know that Navratri starts next week, that Rugra mushrooms are in season in Jharkhand right now, that paneer is ₹40 more expensive this winter, or that the ICMR iron requirement for an anemic teenager is specific. You bake all of this in. They have to be told every single time.

3. It remembers your family
ChatGPT forgets you after the conversation ends. It has no idea your family hated dal khichdi last Tuesday. ParivarSehat's feedback loop makes Week 2 smarter than Week 1. That is personalization that no general AI offers.

4. Voice in real Indian languages
ChatGPT Voice barely handles Hinglish. Sarvam gives your app the ability to work for a rural homemaker who speaks only Bhojpuri or a Tamil grandmother. This is a 700 million person market that global apps are completely ignoring.

5. Complete family workflow, not a chat prompt
ChatGPT gives you text. ParivarSehat gives you: profile → 7-day plan → per-member plates → grocery list in INR → nutrition tracking → feedback → improved Week 2 → daily chat → leftover reuse. The workflow is the product. You can't replicate this experience by prompting ChatGPT.

6. The Harmony Score
No AI gives you a single number that says "this meal plan serves your whole family at 87%." It's visual, explainable, shareable, and uniquely yours.
